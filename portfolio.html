Animation Reel, 2021

My Artist Statement

Hi there! I'm Jon Dorais, or Jonny Yoon Dorais as credited on past productions. Everybody has a story to tell. As a student filmmaker and animator, I lacked money or resources for projects. There had to be a more efficient technique of animation for my team and me. I wanted to create a style that could be fast, affordable, and impactful. To fix this problem, I came up with a computer animation technique that used 2D-looking models in 3D sets. This has become an outlet for multiple award-winning animated short films. It continues to make seemingly impossible ideas possible.

Wacky Towns, 2018-2019

Series Creator, Producer, Lead Animator, Writer, Voice Actor

An award-winning, TV-length pilot about a little town where everyone is encouraged (or forced) to the best person they can be. The animation includes 2D characters in a 3D cel-shaded world.

Two original episodes and one mini-short for a potential TV series. All made from a combined cast and crew of over 20 people - all of whom were students.

Les Is More - Trailer, 2018

Producer, Director, Lead Animator

Trailer for an animated "monster-of-the-week" pilot. In this episode, monster-hunters Les and Chef Sanchez get on the bad side of a giant, talking spider. This short features both 3D and 2D characters on a digitally-made multi-pane camera.

Choco Poca - Trailer, 2017

Series Creator, Animator, Editor

Directorial Debut

Trailer for a complete webseries that blends 2D and 3D animation. It tells the story about Carrotcake, a candy rabbit who finds himself in a chocolate bunny zombie apocalypse. But everything changes when he discovers the chocolate bunny zombies are actually pretty cool, and it's all the other candies that are awful.
